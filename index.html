Haskell Development and Consulting

Reichert Brothers is a Haskell Development and Consulting company based in Houston, Texas. Our primary goal is to provide consulting, mentoring, and support to businesses and organizations using Haskell and other statically-typed languages. We provide a range of high-quality services including bespoke application development, training, system maintenance, integration, and advice.
